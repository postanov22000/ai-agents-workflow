How to Set Up Email Forwarding

Gmail
Outlook
Apple Mail
Yahoo Mail

Step 1: Open Gmail Settings

Click the gear icon in the top right corner of Gmail and select "See all settings".

Step 2: Go to Forwarding Tab

Click on the "Forwarding and POP/IMAP" tab in the settings menu.

Step 3: Add Forwarding Address

Click "Add a forwarding address" and enter: replyzeai@gmail.com

Step 4: Verify Forwarding

Check your email for a verification code from Gmail and enter it to confirm the forwarding address.

Step 5: Enable Forwarding

Select "Forward a copy of incoming mail to" and choose replyzeai@gmail.com from the dropdown menu.

Step 1: Open Outlook Settings

Click the gear icon in the top right corner to open Settings.

Step 2: Navigate to Mail Forwarding

Type "forwarding" in the search box and select "Forwarding" from the results.

Step 3: Enable Forwarding

Check "Enable forwarding" and enter replyzeai@gmail.com as the forwarding address.

Step 4: Save Changes

Click "Save" at the top of the page to apply your changes.

Step 1: Open Apple Mail Preferences

Click "Mail" in the menu bar and select "Preferences".

Step 2: Go to Rules Tab

Click on the "Rules" tab and then click "Add Rule".

Step 3: Create a New Rule

Name your rule (e.g., "Forward to ReplyZe AI"). Set the conditions to apply to all messages.

Step 4: Set Up Forwarding Action

Select "Forward Message" from the actions, and enter replyzeai@gmail.com as the recipient.

Step 5: Save the Rule

Click "OK" to save the rule and start forwarding emails.

Step 1: Open Yahoo Mail Settings

Click the gear icon and select "More Settings".

Step 2: Go to Mailboxes

Select "Mailboxes" from the left sidebar, then click on your email address.

Step 3: Set Up Forwarding

Click "Add forwarding address" and enter replyzeai@gmail.com.

Step 4: Verify Forwarding Address

Check your email for a verification code and confirm the forwarding address.

Step 5: Enable Forwarding

Toggle on "Forwarding" and select the newly added address.
